How to Manage Hotels

  1. Go to Hotel Management in admin panel
  2. Click "Add Hotel" to create a new hotel
  3. Fill in hotel details (name, address, city, contact, star rating)
  4. Add room categories inline with pricing:
    • Room Type (Standard, Deluxe, Suite, etc.)
    • Price per night
    • Extra mattress price
    • Maximum occupancy
  5. Save the hotel
  6. Use these hotels when creating itineraries for leads
